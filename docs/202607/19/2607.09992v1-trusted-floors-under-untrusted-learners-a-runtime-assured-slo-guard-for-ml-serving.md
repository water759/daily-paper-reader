## 摘要
现代ML服务越来越依赖学习型、无界组件（路由器、延迟SLO准入器、准入阶梯）来决定租户的服务质量；当其中一个出错时，保证的SLO可能会悄然失效，而底层的Kubernetes各层（Kueue、DRA、Gateway-API推理扩展）只会增加跨层的意外。与其相信学习器是正确的，我们限制错误学习器可能造成的损害：一个小的可信守护包裹不可信的学习器——学习器提议，守护处置。租户的保证SLO义务分为两部分，具有不同的认识论。其安全投影（每请求的调度可行性以及每类别、每窗口的服务地板）是守护在运行时通过预留和优先级调度执行的可控义务，无论学习型准入器如何任意错误。其聚合义务（尾延迟百分位数）是没有每请求执行点的统计残差；一个廉价的确定性筛选不仅在高饱和度附近，而且在远低于饱和度时都是乐观不成立的。在真实的2xV100上，守护在两种相反方向失败的丢弃策略下，针对学习型准入器的每种错误校准，保持保证类别的未命中率为0.0（10次重复，最坏上限Wilson CI 0.0053）。针对在服务模拟器上部署的真实GAIE流控制，一个错误标注的路由器将相同的保证请求从未命中率0.0翻转为1.0；我们的守护根据真实类别预留，因此标签无法破坏其地板。我们描述了何时可以信任廉价静态筛选，何时不能。作为Frontiers风格的投稿，我们在商用2xV100和服务模拟器上评估了该立场，并将数据中心规模、真实模型流控制以及封闭最坏情况定理作为议程范围。

## Abstract
Modern ML serving increasingly lets learned, unbounded components (routers, latency-SLO admitters, admit ladders) decide a tenant's quality of service; when one is wrong, the assured SLO can silently break, and the Kubernetes layers beneath (Kueue, DRA, the Gateway-API Inference Extension) only add cross-layer surprises. Rather than trust the learner to be right, we bound the damage a wrong one can do: a small trusted guard wraps the untrusted learner -- learned proposes, the guard disposes. A tenant's assured-SLO obligation splits into two parts with different epistemics. Its safety projection (per-request dispatch feasibility and a per-class, per-window service floor) is a controllable obligation the guard enforces at runtime, regardless of an arbitrarily-wrong learned admitter, by reservation and priority dispatch. Its aggregate obligation (a tail-latency percentile) is a statistical residual with no per-request enforcement point; a cheap deterministic screen for it is optimistically unsound not only near saturation but well below it. On real 2xV100 the guard holds assured-class miss 0.0 (reps 10, worst upper Wilson CI 0.0053) across every miscalibration of a learned admitter under two shed policies that fail in opposite directions. Against a real, deployed GAIE Flow Control on a serving simulator, a mislabelling router flips the same assured requests from miss 0.0 to 1.0; our guard reserves by the true class, so the label cannot break its floor. We characterize when a cheap static screen can be trusted and when it cannot. As a Frontiers-style submission we evaluate the stance on commodity 2xV100 and a serving simulator, and scope datacenter scale, real-model Flow Control, and a closed worst-case theorem as the agenda.
